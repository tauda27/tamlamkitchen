Cooking Classes

Join me for hands-on Chinese cooking classes, offered in the comfort of your home or mine. Each session lasts approximately two hours and includes the preparation of 2–3 dishes of your choice, with pricing ranging from $65 to $75 per person, depending on the selected menu. Please choose below:
